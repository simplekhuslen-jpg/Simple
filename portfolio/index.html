About Me

Capturing the Heartbeat of Sport

I'm Uuganerdene, a sports photographer based in Mongolia with years of experience shooting at the highest level — from national basketball finals to international volleyball championships across Asia.

Armed with a Sony telephoto lens and an eye for decisive moments, I cover basketball, volleyball, wrestling, swimming, and every sport in between. My work has been featured at official tournaments and published by national sports federations.

Available for events, athlete campaigns, and brand partnerships.
